Plain-language privacy information.

Website privacy

This website is hosted on GitHub Pages. It does not include analytics, advertising pixels, tracking cookies, or contact forms.

App data

Brimst32 apps are built to keep data use limited to what the app needs to function. If an app uses device permissions, cloud features, or a third-party service, that should be visible in the app experience or its store listing.

Platform providers may collect crash reports or diagnostics according to the choices you make in your device settings.

Support email

If you email support, your message is used to answer your request and diagnose the issue you reported. You can ask for previous support correspondence to be deleted by emailing brimst32@gmail.com.

Contact

Questions about privacy can be sent to brimst32@gmail.com.
