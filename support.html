Support

Help with Brimst32 apps.

For bug reports, feature questions, or account/privacy questions, email brimst32@gmail.com.

What to include

  • The app name and version, if you can find it.
  • Your device model and operating system version.
  • What you expected to happen and what happened instead.
  • Steps to reproduce the problem, plus screenshots when useful.

Response path

I read support mail directly. If the issue is a bug, the most useful report is one that lets me reproduce the behavior on a test device.
